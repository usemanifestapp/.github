## Why Manifest

The best partnerships in crypto still happen in DMs and closed back-channels — invisible, unverifiable, and out of reach for newcomers. Manifest turns *"what you're building toward"* into a structured, public record that the right counterpart can actually find.

## How it works

1. **Declare** — Post a structured intent: partnership, investment, integration, hiring, grants, and more.
2. **Discover** — Manifest routes it to aligned, X-verified counterparts — no impersonators.
3. **Connect** — Reach out with context; contact details are exchanged only after both sides opt in.
4. **Seal** — When both parties confirm a partnership, a soulbound **Proof of Collaboration** is minted to each — a credential no one can self-issue, buy, or transfer.

## What makes it different

- **Structured, not noise** — every intent is typed and queryable, not a freeform post.
- **Identity-verified** — counterparts are verified humans and orgs, cutting impersonation.
- **Proof of Collaboration** — bilateral, onchain, non-transferable proof that a partnership actually happened.
- **Open by design** — the intent layer and its primitives are open, so anyone can verify or build on them.
